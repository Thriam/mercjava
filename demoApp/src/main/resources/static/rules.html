Cricket Rules

Understanding the fundamentals of the gentleman's game

üéØ Test Cricket Basics

Match Duration

Test matches last up to 5 days, with each team batting twice (two innings each). Each day's play consists of 3 sessions of about 2 hours each.

1

Teams

Each team has 11 players. There are two teams: the batting team and the fielding team.

2

The Pitch

A rectangular 22-yard (20.12m) strip in the center of the field. The pitch has three stumps at each end.

3

Wickets

Three wooden stumps topped with two bails. If all three bails are dislodged, the batsman is "out".

4

Overs

An over consists of 6 legal deliveries bowled by one bowler. The bowler changes ends after each over.

üèÉ How to Score Runs

Run Type Description Value
Run Both batsmen run to opposite ends 1-6 runs
Four Ball touches boundary after hitting ground 4 runs
Six Ball crosses boundary without touching ground 6 runs
Bye Runs off delivery without bat contact 1+ runs
Leg Bye Runs off body without bat contact 1+ runs
No Ball Illegal delivery (overstepping, height, etc.) 1 run + free hit
Wide Ball Delivery too wide for batsman 1 run + re-bowl

‚ùå Ways to Get Out

1

Caught

Batsman hits the ball and it's caught by a fielder before touching ground.

2

Bowled

Ball hits the stumps and dislodges the bails.

3

Leg Before Wicket (LBW)

Ball would have hit stumps and batsman didn't offer a shot.

4

Run Out

Fielding team dislodges bails while batsman is out of crease.

5

Stumped

Wicketkeeper dislodges bails while batsman is out of crease.

6

Hit Wicket

Batsman dislodges bails with bat or body while playing.

7

Timed Out

New batsman fails to arrive within 3 minutes.

8

Obstructing the Field

Batsman deliberately obstructs fielders.

9

Hit the Ball Twice

Batsman hits ball twice intentionally.

üéØ ODI Cricket Basics

Match Duration

One Day International (ODI) matches consist of 50 overs per side, typically lasting 6-8 hours.

1

Overs per Innings

Each team gets 50 overs to bat. An over consists of 6 legal deliveries.

2

Powerplay

First 10 overs - only 2 fielders allowed outside 30-yard circle. Overs 11-40: 4 fielders outside. Last 10 overs: 5 fielders outside.

3

Follow-on

In tests, trailing team may be forced to bat again if they trail by 200+ runs (after 1st innings).

üéØ T20 Cricket Basics

Match Duration

Twenty20 (T20) matches consist of 20 overs per side, typically lasting about 3 hours. The fastest format!

1

Overs per Innings

Each team gets 20 overs to bat. Maximum 4 overs per bowler.

2

Fielding Restrictions

First 6 overs: Maximum 2 fielders outside 30-yard circle. Overs 7-20: Maximum 5 fielders outside.

3

Free Hit

After a no-ball, the next delivery is a free hit where the batsman cannot be dismissed except run out.

4

Strategic Timeout

Each team gets one 60-90 second timeout (mandatory in some leagues).
